Our Programs and Courses

DWI Education Class (12 Hours)

Designed for first-time offenders under community supervision. This class includes three 4-hour live Zoom sessions. A completion certificate is provided within 10 days.

Drug Offender Education Program (15 Hours)

Created for individuals charged with drug-related misdemeanors or felonies affecting their driving privileges. This program includes five 3-hour live Zoom classes, with certificates mailed within 5-10 days.

DWI Intervention Program (32 Hours)

Structured specifically for repeat offenders, providing intensive instruction over multiple sessions to meet court requirements.

Victim Impact Panel (2 Hours)

An impactful session featuring speakers who share personal experiences to promote accountability and responsible decision-making.

Substance Abuse Evaluation (SASSI)

A guided evaluation offering comprehensive assessment and supportive recommendations for personal and court-related purposes.
