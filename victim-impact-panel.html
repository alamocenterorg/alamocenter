What Is the Victim Impact Panel?

A Victim Impact Panel (VIP) class is a court-mandated educational session designed to show participants how drunk and drug-impaired driving has affected real victims and their families. The goal is to create awareness, empathy, and accountability by exposing the emotional, physical, and financial consequences of DWI/DUI crashes.
