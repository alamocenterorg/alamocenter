Our Mission and Commitment

At Alamo Center, our mission is helping individuals successfully complete required DWI and Drug Offender Education programs with respect, convenience, and compassion. We uphold rigorous standards that comply fully with Texas law and regulatory guidelines.

Our Court-Ordered Programs Include:

DWI Education Class (12 Hours)

A mandatory program for first-time DWI offenders. This course covers the risks and consequences of impaired driving through three 4-hour Zoom classes. Certificates are provided within 10 days.

Drug Offender Education Program (15 Hours)

Created for individuals charged with drug-related misdemeanors or felonies affecting their driving privileges. This program includes five 3-hour live Zoom classes, with certificates mailed within 5-10 days.

DWI Intervention Program (32 Hours)

Designed for individuals with multiple DWI offenses. This in-depth program offers extended guidance and intervention strategies across multiple sessions.

Victim Impact Panel (2 Hours)

A powerful awareness session featuring speakers who have experienced the consequences of substance-related incidents firsthand. Promotes accountability and behavioral change.

Substance Abuse Evaluation (SASSI)

A court-accepted evaluation that provides a structured assessment for individuals required to complete alcohol or drug screenings. Results are confidential and can be shared with legal entities if requested.
