What Is the Drug Offender Education Program?

The Drug Offender Education Program, also called DOEP, is a 15-hour course mandated by the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation (TDLR). This class is required for individuals with a drug-related conviction, often involving controlled substances, possession charges, or violations related to marijuana, narcotics, or prescription drugs.
